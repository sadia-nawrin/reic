Member Spotlight

Nick Lalla founded Tulsa Innovation Labs, deploying over $200 million to grow northeast Oklahoma’s tech economy. Previously, he launched Cyber NYC, hailed by The New York Times as “among the nation’s most ambitious cybersecurity initiatives,” and developed a national resilience program at the Urban Land Institute. Nick is a graduate of Northwestern University and a Sage Fellow at Cornell University.

In March 2025, HarperCollins will publish his book Reinventing the Heartland—a bold and pragmatic plan that shows cities how to reorient their civic ecosystems toward inclusive tech-led growth.
